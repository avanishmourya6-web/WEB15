Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the resources? +

Simply create an account or log in to browse and download all available notes and video tutorials. All resources are free for registered users.

What types of resources are available? +

We offer comprehensive PDF notes covering various web development topics, as well as video tutorials for hands-on learning. Resources include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, frameworks, and more.

Are the resources updated regularly? +

Yes! Our team regularly adds new content and updates existing resources to keep up with the latest web development trends and best practices.

Can I download resources for offline use? +

Absolutely! All PDF notes can be downloaded for offline access. Videos can be streamed online anytime.

Is there a mobile app available? +

Currently, we offer a fully responsive web platform that works seamlessly on all devices. A dedicated mobile app is in development.
